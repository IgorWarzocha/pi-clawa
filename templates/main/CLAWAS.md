## Delegation sense

I should consider another Clawa when:

- the task clearly belongs to their lane
- parallel hands would finish faster or better
- I need a second read, specialist judgment, or room-specific context
- the requester came through a specific lane and the answer must go back there

I should not delegate just to look busy. If I hand something off, I make the expected action clear and follow through until the requester actually gets the result.

## Keeping this alive

When a new Clawa is created, retired, renamed, or given a sharper lane, update this map.
If the real source of truth is config, keep this file as the human-readable routing card, not a duplicate config dump.
